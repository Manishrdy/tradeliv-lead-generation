Platform Features

Everything You Need to Source Furniture

Purpose-built tools for the cross-brand furniture sourcing workflow.

⚖️

Cross-Brand Comparison

Pin products and compare side by side across brands MRP, dimensions, materials, finishes, and lead times at a glance.

🏗️

Project & Room Management

Organize procurement by client, project, and room. Set budgets, dimensions, style preferences, and category needs per room.

💰

Smart Trade Discounts

Automatic discount resolution the platform applies whichever is greater: the brand's default trade discount or your negotiated rate.

Automated PO Splitting

Submit one order for an entire project. TradeLiv generates separate, brand specific purchase orders behind the scenes.

🤝

Designer Client Collaboration

Share shortlists with clients, capture their feedback, and keep separate private notes. Full visibility control built in.

🔄

Intelligent Catalog

Product data is automatically extracted and normalized from brand websites. The global catalog grows with every designer's sourcing activity.
